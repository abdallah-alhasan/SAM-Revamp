SOUK AL MARFA

A BUSTLING MODERN MARKET ROOTED IN DUBAI’S DEEPEST TRADITIONS

Souk Al Marfa is the largest market in the heart of Dubai located on the Dubai creek, created in partnership between Nakheel Malls and DP World, the leading provider of worldwide smart end-to-end supply chain logistics.

Strategically located north of Dubai in Dubai Islands, Souk Al Marfa is in close proximity to the Dubai airport, Sharjah, and the Northern Emirates.

Its location within the island also connects it directly to Mina Rashid, with the freedom to ship and import directly to its 7,000 shops & pavilions.

Along with several benefits that include 100% foreign ownership, repatriation of capital and profit, logistical ease and ancillary amenities, as well as minimal paperwork, Souk Al Marfa provides affordable growth opportunities to traders.
